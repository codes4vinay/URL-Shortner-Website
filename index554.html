Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is VKR URL Shortener?

A: VKR URL Shortener is a free URL shortening service designed by Vinay Kumar. It allows you to easily shorten long URLs.

Q: How does it work?

A: Simply paste your long URL into the input field and click "Shorten." VKR URL Shortener will generate a short URL and a QR code for your convenience.

Q: Can I customize the short URL?

A: Currently, customization features are not available. Short URLs are automatically generated based on the provided long URL.

Q: Is VKR URL Shortener free to use?

A: Yes, VKR URL Shortener is completely free to use.

Q: How can I contact support?

A: For support or inquiries, you can visit the Contact page on Vinay Kumar's website: Contact.
